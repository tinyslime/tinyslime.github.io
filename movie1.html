Review

The beginning of the movie starts very suddenly. We do not really know much about any of the crew members, not to mention I can barely remember their names! They start to die very abruptly, like we do not know anything about them, so why should we care?

It’s not really all that bad until after the guy commits suicide, as the movie goes into the dump because the bogeyman appears! We have no clue how he got there and no clue how he is alive after 7 years. He starts killing people for no reason.

To make this film better, I would’ve had them explore more about how the other crew died from being mesmerized by the sun, like Searle. Instead of having a random guy appear on the ship, I would’ve made it like the sun drives them crazy.

Is it perfect? Hell no, I really didn’t enjoy watching this film. Don’t waste your time watching this!
